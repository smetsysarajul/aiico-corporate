About Mortgage Protection?

AIICO Mortagage protection plan covers you against the unfortunate event of death during the loan repayment period.

  • Risk is Covered

    A protection plan that covers the risk of death of a mortgagor within the period of repayment of a mortgage loan.

  • Progressive ease

    Sum Assured decreases as loan is repaid.

So why Mortgage Insurance?

  • Full sum assured or reduced sum assured payable on death depending on outstanding loan.

  • No survival benefit.

To the staff

  • It serves as confidence booster to obtain the facility.
  • Protects the interest of his dependants at his demise.

To the family members of the mortgagor

  • Helps to repay all loans outstanding at the point of death of the mortgagor.
  • Helps in the realization of government goals and objectives as it relates to home ownership.

How to Calculate?

  • Calculated by applying rates based on the age next birthday, sum assured and term of the policy.

Would you like to purchase this plan?

Borrowers who require collateral security for a loan. Also lenders and institutions requiring assurance that loan facility will be repaid. Speak to an AIICO Staff or Agent today. You may also reach us via email on aiicontact@aiicoplc.com.
